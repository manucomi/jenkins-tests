## Variants

The HBR logos are added as a property of the `Logo` component and are accessible through `Logo.variants`,

<Table
    headings={['Property', 'Description']}
    body={[
        [
            '<code>Logo.variants.HBP_SHIELD</code>',
            'Harvard Business Publishing logo with shield.',
        ],
        [
            '<code>Logo.variants.HBR_SHIELD</code>',
            'Harvard Business Review logo with shield.',
        ],
        [
            '<code>Logo.variants.HBR_NO_SHIELD</code>',
            'Harvard Business Review logo without shield.',
        ],
    ]}
/>

## Use example

```jsx
import Logo from 'mfe-core/ui/Logo';

return <Logo className={styles.logo} variant={Logo.variants.HBR_SHIELD} />;
```
